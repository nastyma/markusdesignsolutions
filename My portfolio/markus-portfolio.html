Who I Am

Brand identity that resonates and endures.

I'm Markus — a branding and visual identity designer with over six years of experience helping businesses discover their visual voice. My work lives at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics, translating what a brand stands for into systems that speak clearly across every touchpoint.

From startups finding their footing to established businesses ready to evolve, I bring the same rigorous, thoughtful approach to every engagement — because the right identity isn't just beautiful, it's effective.
